1. Overview

श्री रामचरितमानस is a devotional Android application that lets users read, explore, and claim verses (dohas/chaupais) from the Ramcharitmanas. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

  • Google Account information — When you sign in with Google we receive your name, email address, and profile photo via Firebase Authentication. This is used solely to identify you within the app.
  • User-generated content — Verses you claim are stored in Firebase Firestore under your user account so your progress is synced across devices.
  • Usage analytics — Firebase Analytics collects anonymised, aggregated usage data (screens visited, session length) to help us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is included.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and display your name inside the app.
  • To save and sync the verses you have claimed.
  • To understand overall usage patterns and improve app quality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore and Authentication), subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

Your account data and claimed verses are retained as long as you use the app. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at shayshankr@gmail.com. We will delete your data within 30 days of your request.

6. Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Firebase with appropriate security rules. Only authenticated users can read or write their own data.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after any change constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
