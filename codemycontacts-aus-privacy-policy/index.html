Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 June 2026

Overview

CodeMyContacts AUS ("the App") is a contact-management utility for Android that helps users format, deduplicate, and organise their phone contacts for use with Indian (+91) and Australian (+61) numbers. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data We Access

The App requests the READ_CONTACTS and WRITE_CONTACTS permissions in order to:

  • Read your device contacts to detect formatting issues, duplicates, and missing numbers.
  • Write changes back to your contacts (formatting corrections, merges, deletions) only when you explicitly confirm an action.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any contact data.
  • We do not connect to the internet or any external server.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services.
  • We do not store any personal information on our servers — we have none.

All Processing Is On-Device

Every operation — scanning, formatting, duplicate detection, merging — happens entirely on your device. No data ever leaves your phone. Closing the App clears all in-memory state immediately.

User Control

You are always in control. The App never modifies contacts automatically; every change requires your explicit confirmation (a tap or a dialog approval). You can revoke the Contacts permission at any time via Android Settings → Apps → CodeMyContacts AUS → Permissions.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.
